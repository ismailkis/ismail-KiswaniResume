My name is Ismail Kiswani. I grew up in Redlands California and graduated from high school in Redlands. My ambitions and dreams arose from that small town and high school. I was always surrounded by scholars and teachers that pushed me in the right direction of life. From that grew my love for politics and history. I always try to understand different sides of the spectrum and why people tend to choose one side than the other. My fascination in this drove me to major in Political Science Administrative Studies at the University of California Riverside.

I currently work at the local movie theater in Redlands called Studio Movie Grill. Previously, I had worked at the clothing store Marshalls and interned at Redlands Community Hospital. The movie theater and Marshalls both have taught me valuable lessons about customer service, money and how to operate a business. The internship at the hospital gave me an insight on how collaboration is key in professional settings. The internship also taught me some of the technological tools used by medical professionals.

At the University of California Riverside, I study Political Science Administrative Studies with a focus on Finance. Although it has been my first year, I have gained valuable knowledge in the discipline of political science. To further my experience in leadership, I joined the Associated Students of UCR, which is the student government of the university. In the organization, I have learned how communication and organization is key to running a government.
